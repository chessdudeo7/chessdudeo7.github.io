About Me

Hi! I'm Matthew Zhu, a first year Computer Science Student at the University of Waterloo. I graduated from Merivale High School's Class of 2025 as a student in the International Baccalaureate (IB) Programme. I love tutoring students and my specialty is in STEM subjects, particularly mathematics.


I am also a competitive chess player, having played for almost 2/3 of my life. I compete frequently at the national/international level and have been coaching for some time. I love playing the game and look forward to fostering the next generation of young players.

My Vision

I'm passionate about helping students reach their full potential. Whether you're struggling to grasp a concept or aiming to get ahead, I offer tailored sessions to meet your needs in a clear and engaging way.
